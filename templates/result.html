Some tips to prevent Diabetes

Eat healthy foods

Eat a variety of healthy foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Limit foods high in saturated fat, transfat, added sugars, salt and processed foods.

Drink water

Choose water instead of sugary drinks like soda, sports drinks, and fruit juice.

Be physically active

Regular physical activity can help you lose weight and improve your overall health. Try to get atleast 7 hours of sleep each night.

Quit smoking

Smoking is strongly linked to type 2 diabetes, and quitting can reduce your risk over time.

Maintain a healthy weight

Losing weight can reduce your risk of developing type 2 diabetes. The American Diabetes Association recommends that people with prediabetes lose 7-10% of their body weight.
